About Us

What We Do.

Mission Statement

The fraternity professes to create a bond of brotherhood passed down from generation to generation, including lasting relationships between alumni and currently active members.

Internally

Nu Alpha Phi emphasizes brotherhood and promotion of self-development for all of its members, as encouraged by its seven fundamental principles of Brotherhood, Discipline, Leadership, Pride, Professionalism, Service, and Unity. Members are taught to use these principles in everyday life to promote personal growth. These ideals are brought upon prospective members, as well.

Externally

Nu Alpha Phi seeks to promote Asian-awareness, as well as to serve as a liaison between the Greek community and the Asian community. This is done through a supportive network of alumni and matriculated brothers who actively engage in participation in community service, cultural workshops and events, university programs, and various other on-campus student activities.

Chapter Wars

National athletics event!

Nine will enter -- only one will leave victorious. Defend your chapter's honor through some friendly competition with the brothers of our fraternity.

Every year, Nu Alpha Phi organizes a sports olympic, brother against brother in sports like softball, basketball, and volleyball between all nine chapters. Brothers from every generation, young and old, practice to prepare themselves for this annual event.

When chapter pride is on the line, it is the duty of every brother to demonstrate the strength of his own pack. Only one will earn the crown.
